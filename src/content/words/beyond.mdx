# Silence

I embraced it a bit more than in the past. I have [autoplay off](https://www.guidefari.com/autoplay-opinion/) on all my streaming apps, which resulted in me having lots of quiet moments.

Was weird at first, but I grew to enjoy it. This is a big step for someone who's used to having headphones on 90% of the timeðŸ˜„from a perpetual "won't do anything without music" to embracing silent moments felt serene.
