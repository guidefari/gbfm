## [Datasette's Music For Programming](http://musicforprogramming.net/)
A big inspiration for this website comes from Datasette's project. It's one of my favourite corners on the internet, no doubt.
Recently got a UI update too. It's a great place to listen to music while you work. [I've been listening to it for years now](https://www.guidefari.com/playlists-for-coding/).
